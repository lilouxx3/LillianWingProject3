  • Urban Development

    Born and raised in an urban environment, I have learned that cities provide many advantages and opportunities - when we are organized in the right ways. Cities can have overall negative effects on the environment when waste is not treated carefully. On the other hand, cities provide an opportunity for people to consume less and exert less pressure on the environment when we live in dense areas where we do not need to rely on motor vehicles, and where we can live in smaller spaces and thus requiring less energy to heat out homes.
